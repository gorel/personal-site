Quiz

Question 1: What will be printed after running the following program?

result = ""
  if 3 <= 4:
    result += "yes"
  else:
    result += "no"
  print(result)

a. yes
b. no
c. yesno
d. (error)

Question 2: What will be printed after running the following program?

result = ""
  bmi = 24

  if bmi < 18.5:
    result += "underweight"
  if bmi < 25:
    result += "normal"
  if bmi < 30:
    result += "overweight"
  else:
    result += "obese"

  print(result)

a. normaloverweight
b. normal
c. overweight
d. obese
e. (error)

Exercises

  • From section 5.14, do exercises 5.2 and 5.3
  • [movies] Write a program called movies.py that asks the user for their five favorite movies then shows them the list like so:
What is your favorite movie?
  > movie1
  What is your second favorite movie?
  > movie2
  What is your third favorite movie?
  > movie3
  What is your fourth favorite movie?
  > movie4
  What is your fifth favorite movie?
  > movie5

  So your movie rankings are:
  1. movie1
  2. movie2
  3. movie3
  4. movie4
  5. movie5
  • [censor] You're a secret agent! Write a function called censor(text, word) that replaces all occurrences of word in text with CENSORED. After making all relevant replacements, output the final result in the terminal.
    Use this as your input text:
Dear family,

  The president of our government has sent me on a secret mission.
  I flew to Antarctica on a huge airplane to get to my secret location.
  I'm staying in a fancy hotel! Being a secret spy is so fun!
  I really hope the enemy doesn't find this letter, because I reveal so many top-secret details in my letters to you.
  Luckily, I have full faith in our government programmers that they will censor any important details.
  I'll see you soon!

  Love,
  Your favorite spy

Your program should censor the following words:

  • president
  • government
  • Antarctica
  • secret
  • airplane
  • hotel
  • spy
  • enemy
